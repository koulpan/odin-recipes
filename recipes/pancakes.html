Sweet potato pancakes with feta and cherry tomatoes

A bitter-sweet recipe that your guests will love!

An easy and quick recipe, for your cravings, your munchies and much more! The hardest think you will have to do, in order to make that recipe, is find the courage to get off the couch!

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes, medium (for the recipe, you will need 400 g flesh)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 4 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 2 egg yolks, of medium eggs
  • 2 eggs, medium
  • 1 tablespoon(s) mint, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) oregano, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) thyme, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) basil, finely chopped
  • 50 g all-purpose flour
  • 2 egg whites, of medium eggs

To assemble

  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  • 100 g turkey, into slices
  • 50 g rocket
  • 100 g feta cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 tablespoon(s) thyme
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil

Steps

For the sweet potatoes

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350° F) set to fan.
  2. Line a baking pan with parchment paper.
  3. Use a fork to prick the sweet potatoes on all sides and wrap each one with aluminum foil.
  4. Put the sweet potatoes in the pan.
  5. Transfer the pan to the oven and bake the sweet potatoes for 1 to 11⁄2 hours (depending on their size).
  6. Remove the pan from the oven, take out the sweet potatoes, and remove the aluminum foil.
  7. Cut the sweet potatoes horizontally in half, while they are still hot, remove their flesh with a spoon and put it in a bowl.
