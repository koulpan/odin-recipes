Sweet potato pancakes with feta and cherry tomatoes

A bitter-sweet recipe that your guests will love!

An easy and quick recipe, for your cravings, your munchies and much more! The hardest think you will have to do, in order to make that recipe, is find the courage to get off the couch!

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes, medium (for the recipe, you will need 400 g flesh)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 4 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 2 egg yolks, of medium eggs
  • 2 eggs, medium
  • 1 tablespoon(s) mint, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) oregano, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) thyme, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) basil, finely chopped
  • 50 g all-purpose flour
  • 2 egg whites, of medium eggs

To assemble

  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  • 100 g turkey, into slices
  • 50 g rocket
  • 100 g feta cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 tablespoon(s) thyme
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil

Steps

For the sweet potatoes

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350° F) set to fan.
  2. Line a baking pan with parchment paper.
  3. Use a fork to prick the sweet potatoes on all sides and wrap each one with aluminum foil.
  4. Put the sweet potatoes in the pan.
  5. Transfer the pan to the oven and bake the sweet potatoes for 1 to 11⁄2 hours (depending on their size).
  6. Remove the pan from the oven, take out the sweet potatoes, and remove the aluminum foil.
  7. Cut the sweet potatoes horizontally in half, while they are still hot, remove their flesh with a spoon and put it in a bowl.
  8. For the recipe, you will need 400 g of the flesh. Store the leftover flesh in the refrigerator for future use (for example, to accompany a dish or to puree it).

For the pancakes

  1. Put the 400 g sweet potato flesh in a second bowl and crush it with a fork.
  2. Add salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons of olive oil, and mix well with the fork.
  3. Add the 2 egg yolks, 2 eggs, and the finely chopped fresh herbs, and mix.
  4. Add the flour and mix until it is incorporated into the mixture.
  5. Whisk the egg whites into a bowl until they are fluffy and you have a nice meringue.
  6. Add the meringue into the mixture with the eggs and fold gently with a silicone spatula.
  7. Place a frying pan over medium heat and add 1 teaspoon of olive oil.
  8. Use a tablespoon to take a little of the batter and add it to the pan. Add 4 tablespoons in total and make sure to leave some space between the pancakes.
  9. Cook the pancakes for 2 minutes on one side, flip them over, and cook them for another 2 minutes -or as long as needed- until they are cooked through.
  10. Remove the pancakes from the pan and put them on a plate with some paper towels.
  11. Wipe the pan clean with paper towels and follow the same process for the whole pancake batter.

To assemble

  1. Put the pancakes on a plate, add the cherry tomatoes on top along with the turkey, rocket, and feta cheese crumbled with your hands, and sprinkle with some salt, pepper, thyme, and olive oil.
  2. Serve
