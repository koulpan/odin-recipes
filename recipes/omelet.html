Omelet tortilla wrap

The best omelet you have ever eaten!

Just like a simple omelet, this recipe is fast and really easy to do, but incomparably delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 teaspoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 tortilla
  • 2 slices smoked turkey
  • 2 slices cheese
  • cucumber
  • cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Place a frying pan over high heat and add the olive oil.
  2. In a bowl add the eggs, salt, and pepper, and whisk them well until they are smooth.
  3. Transfer the eggs to the hot pan and spread the tortilla over them. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the omelet is cooked through.
  4. PFlip the omelet and tortilla over and spread the deli turkey and cheese slices over them. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Wrap into a roll and serve.
