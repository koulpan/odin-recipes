Quick smoked tuna canapés

A quick, gourmet recipe for even the most difficult judges!

One of the best recipes to make, when expecting guests and having an empty fridge!

Ingredients

  • 6 slices sandwich bread, crustless
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/4 bunch parsley
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 80 g tuna, smoked

To serve

  • parsley
  • slices lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350° F) set to fan.
  2. Use a rolling pin to flatten the sandwich bread slices on your worktop in order to make them thinner.
  3. Use your hands to wrap each bread slice into a cone and seal the cone at the bottom with a toothpick.
  4. Crumple some parchment paper pieces into small balls and fill each cone with a crumpled ball to help it keep its shape during baking.
  5. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and put the bread cones in it.
  6. Drizzle the cones with the olive oil, sprinkle them with some salt and pepper, put the pan in the oven, and bake the bread cones for 10 minutes.
  7. Remove the pan from the oven and discard the crumpled parchment paper balls.
  8. Finely chop the onion, parsley, and tomato on a chopping board.
  9. Fill the cones with the cream cheese, the finely chopped veggies, and the smoked tuna.
  10. Serve with parsley and lemon slices.
